He handed me a laptop, and it took me a while to remember what “SP” meant. My father had explained it to me before. “SP” was an abbreviation for the ancient Roba word “supernatura1poer,” which meant natural power.

It was a universal standard used worldwide for measuring individuals with extraordinary abilities, just like the Roba language itself, which had universal applicability worldwide.

When my father explained it to me, my interest was focused on the question of “foreigners practicing martial arts,” almost causing me to forget this basic term.

For someone like me, influenced by the Eastern martial arts system, the grading criteria for SP levels seemed vague and crude. It simply categorized individuals with supernatural powers into twelve levels based solely on their strength, with three levels making up one rank: Low Rank, Middle Rank, High Rank, and Special Rank. The classification appeared simple and easy to understand, but the actual differences in power were immense.

This is almost inconceivable in the Eastern martial arts system! Factors like aura, temperament, and strategy, the “soft conditions,” were not considered in this standard. However, this standard was established more than 3,800 years ago, and considering the complexity of human brains at that time, it’s not surprising!

I never thought that I would need to use this grading standard here. My father had never mentioned what level I currently held. In reality, this registration for exceptional individuals from other regions was for the convenience of managing public order and handling various affairs for people like us… After some thought, I decided to fill in my identity, just a name, it shouldn’t hurt.

The person looked at my name, widened his mouth in surprise, and then looked at me. “So, you’re Zhang Zhenyu. It’s a pleasure to meet you. I’m Han Zhongshan from ‘Dao Hai Men.’ If you ever need anything, feel free to contact me; I’m usually here.”

I thought for a moment and smiled. “I’ve met Han Mingxin from your sect before. His water-walking skills are impressive!”

Han Zhongshan smiled warmly and mentioned that Han Mingxin was his nephew and the top talent among the newcomers in the sect. He had achieved a good ranking in the recent East Sea Martial Arts Tournament.

After a brief conversation, I finally started filling out the form. Han Zhongshan’s response was swift, and he smiled as he escorted me out. He left a positive impression, and at the very least, he had sharp insight!

My father’s instructions were now complete, and I had nothing left to do. I needed to meet up with Su Yi, but…

As I stepped out of the shop, I suddenly couldn’t tell which way was which. I walked a few steps along the crowded street in a daze, thinking I should be near the entrance of the Spring and Autumn Pavilion, but when I looked up, I was taken aback. “Where… am I?”

“I never thought your son would turn out to have no sense of direction!”

Two middle-aged men, thousands of miles apart, simultaneously enjoyed the same amusing scene. The live video recording obtained through the use of high-precision military satellites amid their busy schedules was quite precious. So, one of them took the opportunity to tease his lifelong friend, while the other just smirked. “Are you happy to have my directionally challenged son hanging around your perfect daughter?”

His voice trembled slightly, clearly less stable and smooth than the other person’s end of the call. Zhang Yunwang appeared somewhat frustrated as he raised his voice a bit more, but doing so only intensified the artillery and bombing sounds on his side. Nevertheless, this allowed the two men, who had been at odds for a long time, to finally get to the point.

“After the plane landed safely, the Forbidden has already dispatched forces here… It’s very secretive, and that kind of power doesn’t seem like it’s just for attending a local conference, does it?”

“Isn’t that even better? Let my directionally challenged son join in the fun. This way, Su Lao’er, you’ll finally get your wish fulfilled… Just remember to pay my son a commission in the future!”

“It’s a deal. If your directionally challenged son really has the guts to pursue my daughter, you’ll also have to take good care of him in the future…”

Two mature and handsome men laughed coldly together, their laughter sending chills through each other’s hearts…
