Prologue

A planet with advanced life revolves in the vast and boundless universe. The only group of advanced life on the planet calls itself human beings!

Awakening from the primitive age, time has evolved over tens of millions of years, and after three consecutive mythological ages of “worshiping gods”, “destroying gods” and “creating gods”, human beings have entered a new era. Leaving aside, human beings grasped the sharp weapon of “science”, cut off the power of instinct absorbed in them, and stepped into a new era of high development.

Since the new era, “tools” have been the magic weapon for human beings to survive and succeed.

The seven oceans cover more than 90% of the planet’s surface, but the vast land of hundreds of millions of square kilometers is still enough to provide life and food for more than ten billion people-this is the land bestowed by God.

——The famous geographer Geek However, since the outbreak of the First World War in 1434 in the New Era, this sentence has really become a joke among politicians-is it still enough?

How can we fight when enough is enough? The desert, which accounts for one-fifth of the world’s total land area, has caused food crises in the world one after another. The first, second, third… The seventh world war ended a hundred years ago. At that moment, the desert area grew again , reached the top three out of ten!

The land is cut into two parts by the Pole Sky Ocean and the Scorpio Ocean running through the north and south poles: East and West.

The mountains and rivers are vertical and horizontal, and the rivers and seas are rushing, and the two lands are divided into many small pieces, which are called the sky and nine continents, five in the east and four in the west. There is a little difference in numbers, but the difference in area is almost one-third. Since then, the jealous Westerners have been coveting the rich land in the East, and it has not changed until now.

A hundred years ago, the Seventh World War pushed Jianluo Kingdom to the position of world hegemony, but other world powers such as the ancient Yanhuang Kingdom, the East-West Luoba Alliance, and the Heitianzhou Small United Nations were not to be outdone. Checking and balancing the Kingdom of Jianluo——Thanks to nuclear weapons, after having this ultimate weapon that can blow up the planet thousands of times, the world has entered a period of relative stability and peace…

Don’t doubt that the war on the Long Chain Peninsula is just a politician’s farce, really!

The ice sheet, a barren polar continent, Heitian Continent and East Pole Continent are separated from the main continental plate by the Brahma Ocean and Tianzhu Ocean, and there are many small countries forming an alliance of their own.

On this planet, there seems to be no shortage of wars. Since the primitive times, everyone has been fighting and killing without stopping for a moment. Until modern times, there is still no stopping for a moment.

The last time was twenty-five years ago, on September 1, 2026, a war broke out on the Long Chain Peninsula. It was called the “Second Long Chain War” at the beginning, and later it was changed to For the “local war in the East”.

With the Jianluo people from the other side of the ocean, more than a dozen countries in the eastern world took a warm bath together, and then contributed to the family planning in the world: five million people just disappeared!

What about this time?

No one knows, and no one wants to know. Everyone only knows that except for the two parties involved, all other countries have raised their flags to avoid war and shouted “Long live peace, war is guilty” internationally-it seems that this time it is better!

Good people pray to God in this way. Of course, there are also some people who wish the war to last as long as possible, kill as many people as possible, and use more expensive weapons—these are arms dealers, a kind of garbage among human beings. They die and no one mourns.

Is this still a terrorist organization? The world has a new understanding of the criteria for judging the “world police”.

The oriental race is disappearing one after another in a way that the world can’t imagine, stepping on the waves of water, flying into the sky and escaping from the ground, and the soft whistling sound blows up white marks one after another on the calm sea.

Farther away, there were also some people scattered around, holding their breath, and watched the two thousand people leave with complicated eyes. When the crowd dispersed, they breathed out and followed them away, either stepping on water or flying into the sky , the way of leaving does not seem to be inferior to those people just now.
