The last hero Prologue A planet with advanced life revolves in the vast and boundless universe. The only group of advanced life on the planet calls itself human beings! Awakening from the primitive age, time has evolved over tens of millions of years, and after three consecutive mythological ages of “worshiping gods”, “destroying gods” and “creating gods”, human beings have entered a new era. Leaving aside, human beings grasped the sharp weapon of “science”, cut off the power of instinct absorbed in them, and stepped into a new era of high development.
