I found the imitation Tang Priest and imitation Pig and kil ed the pair of them. They were both monkeys. Then I went inside and found the bundles. Nothing’s missing—I’ve checked them over. So I came straight back by wind. What happened to the two Monkeys?”

The Bodhisat va then told him how the Tathagata had told the two of them apart. The idiot was delighted and gave thanks at great length. Master and disciples then said farewel to the Bodhisat va, who went back to the sea.

The four of them were now once more of a single wil and a single mind; al grievances had been washed away and anger removed. They thanked their hosts in the thatched cot age, retied the luggage, saddled up the horse, and headed West. Indeed:

A separation on the journey muddled the Five Elements; At the demon-subduing gathering enlightenment returned.

The spirit returned to the house of the mind and dhyana was calmed; When the six perceptions were control ed the elixir was completed.
